Daiquiri

A quick fresh cocktail anyone can enjoy!

An extremely simple fresh and delicious drink everyone should know!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 oz light rum (your choice)
  • 3/4 oz freshly squeezedlime juice
  • 1/2 to 3/4 simple syrup, to taste
    •

    Steps

    1. Gather ingredients
    2. Add the ingredients to a cocktail shaker
    3. Shake well
    4. Pour into a chilled cocktail glass. Serve and enjoy.
