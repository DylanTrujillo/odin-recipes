Old Fashion

A simple classic whiskey cocktail!

Ingredients

  • 1 sugar cube
  • 2 dashes Angostura bitters
  • 1 oz rye or burbon
  • Orange twist (optional)
    •

    Preparation

    1. Muddle the sugar cube and bitters with one bar spoon of water at the bottom of a chilled rocks glass
    2. Add one large ice cube, or three or four small cubes
    3. Stir until properly diluted, about 30 seconds
    4. Slip orange twist on the side of the cube
